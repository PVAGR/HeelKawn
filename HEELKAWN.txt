✅ UI display: Active mood event and crisis level (Low/Moderate/HIGH/CRITICAL)
✅ Mood affects work behavior: strikes at crisis level > 80%

Mood event types:
- Joy: +50 mood impact (eating meat, accomplishment)
- Sorrow: -80 mood impact (death of colony members)
- Stress: -40 mood impact (mining injuries)
- Triumph: +100 mood impact (hunting animals)
- Despair: -150 mood impact (crisis situation)
- Contentment: +30 mood impact (normal work completion)
- Boredom: -20 mood impact (repetitive tasks)
- Dread: -60 mood impact (combat/danger sensing)

Crisis level system:
- Calculated from mood + despair events
- Low: < 30% (pawns work normally)
- Moderate: 30-60% (minor issues)
- HIGH: 60-80% (work slowdowns)
- CRITICAL: > 80% (pawns go on strike)
